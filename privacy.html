Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 · Effective date: [PLACEHOLDER: set the effective date when this policy is finalized and published]

Arc90 is a personal habit and momentum tracker. The current PWA stores your challenge, habits, logs, protocol notes, and settings on your device using browser storage.

Data Stored On Your Device

Your habit logs, health entries, protocol tracker entries, focus sessions, AI settings, and app preferences are stored locally on the device/browser you use. If you clear browser data or switch devices without exporting a backup, that data may be lost.

Analytics

Arc90 uses Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate product usage — page views and anonymous feature events (for example, "share opened"). These events never include your habit logs, journal entries, health data, or other personal content; that data stays on your device. Vercel processes analytics under its own privacy terms. Blocking analytics with a content blocker does not affect the app.

Push Reminders

If you enable reminder notifications, Arc90 stores a push delivery address (a browser-generated push endpoint) together with your reminder rhythm, time, and timezone offset so scheduled nudges can reach your device when the app is closed. No habit, journal, health, or other personal content is ever stored on the server or included in notifications. Turning reminders off deletes this registration.

Payments

Premium checkout is handled by Stripe. Arc90 does not store full payment card numbers. Stripe may process payment details, billing details, and transaction metadata under its own privacy terms. To keep Premium unlocked across sessions, Arc90 stores a minimal entitlement record on our server (via Supabase): your Stripe customer ID, the email used at checkout, and your subscription status. This record is used only to verify your purchase and is never sold or used for advertising.

Email Updates (Marketing)

If you opt in to Arc90 updates on the landing page or in the app, we store your email address, a consent flag, the consent timestamp, and the signup source in our database (Supabase). We only send product updates you opted in to, and every email will include an unsubscribe link. You can withdraw consent at any time by using that link or by emailing michael28gh@gmail.com, and we will remove your address from the list. We do not sell or share the email list.

AI Connections

If you connect an AI provider key inside Arc90, prompts you send to the coach may be sent directly from your device to that provider. Your key is stored locally on your device. Do not enter sensitive medical, financial, or emergency information into AI chat.

Health And Protocol Information

Arc90 is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Protocol, supplement, peptide, weight, water, and step tracking are self-reported wellness logs, unless you enable the optional Apple Health sync in the native iPhone app (described below).

Apple Health (HealthKit) — Native iOS App

The native Arc90 iPhone app can, only with your explicit permission through Apple's Health permission sheet, read data from Apple Health to fill your daily dashboard: steps, weight, sleep, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV), VO2 max, dietary water, active energy, exercise time, walking/running distance, flights climbed, blood oxygen (SpO2), and respiratory rate.

  • HealthKit access is read-only. Arc90 does not write to Apple Health today.
  • Health data read from HealthKit stays on your device. It is never uploaded to our servers, never included in analytics or notifications, and never shared with anyone.
  • We do not use HealthKit data for advertising or marketing, and we never sell it or disclose it to data brokers, advertising platforms, or any other third party.
  • You can revoke Arc90's Health access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices (or in the Health app), and Arc90 will simply stop receiving that data.

  • Push reminder registration: turning reminders off deletes your push endpoint and reminder schedule from our server.
