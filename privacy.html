Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Arc90 is a personal habit and momentum tracker. The current PWA stores your challenge, habits, logs, protocol notes, and settings on your device using browser storage.

Data Stored On Your Device

Your habit logs, health entries, protocol tracker entries, focus sessions, AI settings, and app preferences are stored locally on the device/browser you use. If you clear browser data or switch devices without exporting a backup, that data may be lost.

Analytics

Arc90 uses Vercel Analytics to understand aggregate product usage — page views and anonymous feature events (for example, "share opened"). These events never include your habit logs, journal entries, health data, or other personal content; that data stays on your device. Vercel processes analytics under its own privacy terms. Blocking analytics with a content blocker does not affect the app.

Push Reminders

If you enable reminder notifications, Arc90 stores a push delivery address (a browser-generated push endpoint) together with your reminder rhythm, time, and timezone offset so scheduled nudges can reach your device when the app is closed. No habit, journal, health, or other personal content is ever stored on the server or included in notifications. Turning reminders off deletes this registration.

Payments

Premium checkout is handled by Stripe. Arc90 does not store full payment card numbers. Stripe may process payment details, billing details, and transaction metadata under its own privacy terms.

AI Connections

If you connect an AI provider key inside Arc90, prompts you send to the coach may be sent directly from your device to that provider. Your key is stored locally on your device. Do not enter sensitive medical, financial, or emergency information into AI chat.

Health And Protocol Information

Arc90 is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Protocol, supplement, peptide, weight, water, and step tracking are self-reported wellness logs unless a future native HealthKit bridge is enabled by you.
