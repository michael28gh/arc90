# Arc90 Legal-Readiness Audit

> **This document is not legal advice.** It is a generated gap analysis to prepare for a review with a qualified attorney. Every "Draft added" item below is a template, not a finished legal document. Do not launch paid subscriptions or submit the HealthKit-enabled iOS build to the App Store without attorney review of `privacy.html`, `terms.html`, and the decisions marked `[PLACEHOLDER]` in both files.

Audited: 2026-07-15. Scope: `privacy.html`, `terms.html`, `landing.html`, `api/*.js`, `ios/App/App/Info.plist`, `ios/App/App/Arc90HealthPlugin.swift`, targeted greps of `js/app.js`.

Product context (verified in repo): local-first PWA + Capacitor iOS app; no accounts; Vercel hosting + anonymous Vercel Analytics; Web Push registration (endpoint + reminder schedule) in Supabase; opt-in marketing email capture in Supabase; Stripe "Founding Premium" $49/yr web subscription with a server-side entitlement record (Stripe customer ID + checkout email + status in Supabase, `api/stripe-webhook.js`); read-only HealthKit integration on iOS (`Arc90HealthPlugin.swift`, `toShare: nil`); BYO AI keys stored locally, prompts sent device → provider.

4. **App Store readiness** — nutrition labels, privacy/EULA URLs, and HealthKit-specific privacy language were not in place; rejection risk for the iOS build. (Privacy language now drafted; Connect metadata still to do.)
