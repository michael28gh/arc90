# /tiktok-creator-outreach

Prepare three personalized TikTok creator outreach drafts for Arc90.

## Steps
1. Read `docs/launch-content-pack.md`.
2. Route to `arc90-tiktok-creator-outreach-agent`.
3. Find or evaluate three creators relevant to habits, discipline, routines, productivity, student focus, or 90-day challenges.
4. Draft one short personalized message per creator.

## Output
Return:
- Creator handle
- Why this creator fits Arc90
- Recent relevant content or positioning
- Draft message
- Offer type
- Manual-send status

## Guardrails
Do not send messages automatically. Do not imply a paid deal if no budget is approved. Do not use mass-DM wording.
