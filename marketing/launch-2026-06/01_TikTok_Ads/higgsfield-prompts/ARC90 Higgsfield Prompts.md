# ARC90 Higgsfield Prompts

## Export Settings
- Format: MP4.
- Aspect ratio: vertical 9:16.
- Length: 5-8 seconds for organic tests.
- Resolution: at least 720x1280.
- Keep all important text inside TikTok safe zones.
- Do not add extra text in Higgsfield. Add captions/text overlays later in TikTok, CapCut, or the editor.
