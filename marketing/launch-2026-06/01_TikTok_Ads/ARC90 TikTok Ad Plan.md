# ARC90 TikTok Ad Plan

## Campaign Goal
- Primary goal: get free starters into ARC90.
- Primary audience: self-improvement users who want better habits, focus, routines, and visible momentum.
- Primary destination: https://arc90.vercel.app/
- Secondary context page: https://arc90.vercel.app/landing
- First launch mode: organic posting plus small paid tests on winners.

## Positioning
ARC90 is not "another habit tracker." It is a 90-day momentum system:
- One real goal.
- Daily reps.
- Visible momentum.
- A system that keeps going after motivation fades.

Keep the first campaign focused on habits, focus, consistency, privacy, and momentum. Avoid protocol, supplement, biomarker, medical, weight-loss, or body-change claims in TikTok ads.

## Organic Posting Rhythm
- Week 1: post 2-3 videos per day.
- Rotate angles instead of posting the same promise repeatedly.
- Pin the clearest explainer or highest-performing hook.
- Reply to useful comments with follow-up videos.

## Paid Test Rhythm
- Wait 48 hours for organic signal.
- Boost the top 6 videos with a small budget.
- Kill weak ads quickly if the hook does not hold attention.
- Make 5 new variants for each winner by changing only the first visual, first sentence, or creator style.

## Compliance Guardrails
- Say: habit tracker, 90-day system, focus, daily reps, momentum, private, local-first.
- Avoid: medical advice, dosing, treatment, body transformation promises, guaranteed results, weight-loss claims.
- If wellness appears, frame it as personal tracking only.
