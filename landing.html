Questions

Good to know

Do I need an account?

No. Arc90 is local-first — your goals, habits, and health data live on your device, not our servers.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything stays on your device, and your health data is never used for ads. Export a backup anytime.

Is this medical or dosing advice?

No. The Protocol tracker records your routine, reminders, symptoms, and notes only. Arc90 never recommends doses or treatment — always follow a licensed healthcare professional.

Can I cancel?

Yes, anytime. Founding Premium is a yearly launch price for early users with no lock-in.

How do I get it on my iPhone?

Open Arc90 in Safari, tap Share, then “Add to Home Screen” for the full-screen app. A native App Store version is on the way.
